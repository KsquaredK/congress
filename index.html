Congresswoman Lauren Underwood

Democrat, 14th District, Illinois

Underwood is the representative for Illinois’s 14th congressional district and is a Democrat. She has served since Jan 3, 2019. Underwood is next up for re-election in 2022 and serves until Jan 3, 2023.

Underwood has cosponsored 2 bills introduced in the current Congress by Republican legislators who fomented the terrorist attack on the Capitol on January 6, 2021 by calling for entire states to be disenfranchised in the 2020 presidential election.
